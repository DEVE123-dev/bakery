About Us!

"Made with Love"

Welcome to Cathlyn's Baked Goods, where every treat is crafted with love, passion, and a sprinkle of magic.

Founded by Cathlyn Kara, a talented baker with a heart full of dreams and a commitment to quality, our bakery is more than just a place for desserts—it's a haven for joy and togetherness.

Cathlyn's journey began with a simple desire to share her love of baking with friends and family.

From perfecting the fluffiest cupcakes to mastering the crunchiest cookies, her creations are inspired by warmth and tradition.

At Cathlyn's Baked Goods, we believe every bite tells a story, and every visit feels like home. Whether you're here for our signature pastries or our classic breads, we’re excited to make your day a little sweeter.
